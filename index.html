Welcome to OSEEC

The Open-source Social Engineering Education Course

As you read the words “social engineering” what thoughts of images does that manifest?

If you’re not a hacker or IT professional you might think of politics, manipulation, or the Cold War. Indeed some of the concepts behind these thoughts here are adjacent to what we mean in the information security world when we say “social engineering”. SE in our context is:

Exploiting human psychology to gain access to unauthorized access, data or information

Social engineering has and always will be the most common attack vector. “Verizon’s analysis of 1600 cybersecurity incidents and 800 breaches found that phishing was involved in 90% of successful attacks.” -https://www.graphus.ai/verizon-says-phishing-still-drives-90-of-cybersecurity-breaches/

The only way to combat this threat is through awareness training and testing.

This course was conceptualized, designed and implemented with the following principles as major motivations: open-source education, accessibility, and our passion for social engineering.

Open-source education: knowledge should be freely available to those who seek it.

Accessibility: this course should be just as useful for a college teacher, technical trainer, or self study.

Passion: The authors have dedicated their lives to the study and analysis of social engineering.
